Our Products

Red Onion Seeds

High-yield variety, suitable for all soil types. These seeds produce deep red bulbs with excellent storage quality.

Yellow Onion Seeds

Popular for its long shelf life and quality bulbs. These white onions have a mild flavor and are perfect for salads.

Hybrid Onion Seeds

Best choice for commercial farming with disease resistance. These hybrids offer uniform size and higher yields.
